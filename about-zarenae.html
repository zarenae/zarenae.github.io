Zarenae is a fast rising singer/songwriter, born and raised in sunny Los Angeles, California. The young artist made her debut in 2020 with a faithful cover of Madonna’s Pop classic “True Blue.” With that track we were introduced to a talented singer with a versatile voice and a knack for expertly delivering a legendary Pop hook. Now she’s back with a smash that will make the world want to dance on her new song “Body Talk”. She shines in a real way on this amazing record with a digestible, that fans everywhere can vibe to for every second she brings us. The vocals ride the beat perfectly, and let her presence be felt with enjoyable lyrics that helps sell the song to the fullest, as well as showcase her star presence that shines bright in it’s finest hour. Zarenae’s next single, Dreamy, is being released on July 30th 2021.
