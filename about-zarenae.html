Zarenae is a fast-rising singer/songwriter, born and raised in sunny Southern California. The young artist made her debut in 2020 with a faithful cover of Madonna’s Pop classic “True Blue.” With that track we were introduced to a talented singer with a versatile voice and a knack for expertly delivering a legendary Pop hook. Now she’s back with a smash that will make the world want to dance on her new song “Body Talk”. She shines in a real way on this amazing record with a digestible, that fans everywhere can vibe to for every second. The vocals ride the beat perfectly, and let her presence be felt with enjoyable lyrics that help sell the song to the fullest, as well as showcase her star presence that shines bright in its finest hour. Zarenae stated in a recent interview, "My goal, whether it’s in a 3-minute song or a two-hour set, is to help people forget everything going on around them, so they can have fun and dance!" The Pop/R&B singer has played all the biggest clubs in Los Angeles and now she’s dazzling audiences around the world with her debut R&B single “You.” Two years ago, she started working to develop the new sound that’s becoming her signature style. Zarenae says “You” is emblematic of the new direction she’s taking with her music.“It’s a transition into everything else I’m creating. It’s R&B with a pop flair and kind of sultry. It feels like I’ve grown as an artist. I love the sound I’m getting in the studio,” she says. “There was no limit to my imagination when I was listening to music. I wanted to write my own music to give people that feeling as well.” Zarenae has a catalog of tracks and music videos she’s gearing up to release over 2022.
